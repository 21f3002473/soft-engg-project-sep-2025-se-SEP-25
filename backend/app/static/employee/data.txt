HR POLICIES AND COURSE DATA

====================================================
1. HR POLICIES
====================================================

1. Leave Policy
1.1 Types of Leave
- Casual Leave (CL): 12 days/year
- Sick Leave (SL): 12 days/year
- Earned Leave (EL): 15 days/year
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks
- Paternity Leave: 10 days
- Compensatory Off: As approved

1.2 Applying for Leave
- Apply via Employee Portal → Leave Requests.
- At least 2 working days in advance except SL.
- HR responds within 48 hours.

1.3 Approval Criteria
- Team workload
- Availability
- Past leave history
- Project deadlines

1.4 Unplanned Sick Leave
- Inform manager before 10 AM
- Medical certificate if >2 days


2. Remote Work Policy
2.1 Eligibility
- Minor illness
- Emergencies
- Light-duty tasks

2.2 Process
- Submit WFH request
- HR may ask justification

2.3 Limit
- 6 WFH days/month


3. Attendance Policy
- Working hours: 9 AM–6 PM
- Late >3 times/month → HR review
- Arrival after 1 PM → Half Day


4. Reimbursement Policy
4.1 Eligible Categories
- Travel
- Food (official meetings)
- Hotel (approved)
- Training (approved)

4.2 Submission
- Upload bills
- Submit within 15 days

4.3 Approval
- HR verifies within 7 days


5. Transfer Policy
- Eligible after 6 months
- Submit through Transfer Request
- Criteria: skill match, vacancies, workload


6. Learning & Development Policy
- Mandatory quarterly training
- Course completion tracked
- GenAI recommendations


7. Performance Review Policy
- Mid & end-quarter reviews
- Parameters: tasks, attendance, deliverables, collaboration, learning
- Auto-generated summaries via GenAI


8. HR FAQ Policy
- FAQs include leave, reimbursements, dress code, code of conduct, IT policies
- Updated quarterly


9. Code of Conduct
- Maintain professionalism
- Prohibited: harassment, data misuse, attendance tampering


10. Announcement Policy
- Only HR/PM can publish
- Types: meetings, holidays, policy updates, training
